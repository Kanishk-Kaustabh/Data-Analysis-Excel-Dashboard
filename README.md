## objective
“To apply my analytical skills in transforming raw data into meaningful insights.

## Questions 

Which year recorded the highest total sales?

Which month had the maximum sales?

Which region contributed the most to total sales?

Which country had the highest number of orders?

Which region shows declining sales?

Which region or country has the highest average order value?

Who are the top 10 customers by revenue?

Which product is purchased the most?

What is the overall customer growth trend?

What is the total revenue compared to the previous year?

## Process

Verify data for any missing values and anomalies, and sort out the same.

Made sure data is consistent and clean with respect to data type, data format and values used.

Created pivot tables according to the questions asked.

Merge all pivot tables into one dashboard and apply slicer to make dynamic.

## Project Insights

Sales increased year over year, with the latest year showing the strongest revenue performance.

November and December showed peak sales due to seasonal demand.

Q4 generated the highest sales, making it the best-performing quarter overall.

The United States region recorded the highest sales.

Top-performing countries displayed higher average order values and consistent monthly growth.

Returning customers contributed significantly to overall sales, indicating strong customer loyalty.

Profit margins were highest in premium product categories, especially high-end bikes.

KPIs showed positive month-over-month and year-over-year growth, especially in total revenue and total orders.

Dashboard visualizations revealed clear trends, seasonality patterns, regional comparisons, and product performance gaps.

## Final Conclusion

This project provides a clear understanding of Adventure Works’ overall sales performance. Using data cleaning, modeling, and visualization in Excel, key insights were identified—showing steady year-on-year growth, strong monthly patterns, and clear differences across regions and product categories. The analysis highlights top-performing markets, customer trends, and areas where sales can be improved, helping support better business decisions. Overall, the project effectively converts raw data into meaningful and actionable insights.
